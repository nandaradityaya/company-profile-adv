About Advantage

Advantage SCM is a national private company engaged in cash management and services, supported by professional and experienced staff to provide the best service results to clients. We have been operating for more than 20 years since 2004 which has enabled us to always provide optimal service.

Advantage SCM helps the banking industry and sub-clients in optimizing operational business activities by preventing duplication of money so as to increase productivity. We always prioritize partner confidentiality and security.
